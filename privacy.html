Privacy Policy

At JobVacancies.com, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data submitted to us through our website.

What Personal Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:


1. Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
2. Resume and Profile Information: Resume, cover letter, work experience, education, skills, and other information related to job searches.
3. Job Search History: Search queries, job listings viewed, and other job search-related activities.
4. Login Credentials: Username, password, and other login credentials.

How We Use Personal Data

We use personal data for the following purposes:


1. Job Search: To provide job search results, job recommendations, and other job search-related services.
2. Employer Connections: To connect job seekers with potential employers and facilitate communication between them.
3. Improving Services: To improve our services, including our job search algorithms, user experience, and customer support.
4. Marketing: To send marketing communications, including newsletters, job alerts, and promotional offers.

How We Share Personal Data

We share personal data with the following third parties:


1. Employers: We share job seeker profiles and resumes with potential employers.
2. Partners: We share personal data with partners who help us provide services, such as job search platforms and career resources.
3. Service Providers: We share personal data with service providers who help us maintain and improve our website, such as hosting providers and analytics services.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about user behavior on our website. This includes:


1. Session Cookies: To track user sessions and maintain login credentials.
2. Analytics Cookies: To track user behavior, including page views, search queries, and job listings viewed.
3. Advertising Cookies: To track user behavior and deliver targeted advertising.

User Rights

You Have The Rights

Users have the following rights:


1. Deletion: To delete their personal data.
2. Opt-out: To opt-out of marketing communications and other services.
3. Correction: To correct and update their personal data.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes:


1. Encryption: We encrypt personal data in transit and at rest.
2. Firewalls: We use firewalls to protect our website from unauthorized access.
3. Access Controls: We implement access controls to restrict access to personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this policy at any time without prior notice. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
