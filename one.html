Ace Talent Consulting is a Recruitment agency providing talent solutions to companies in the Energy, Engineering, Marine and Construction Industries.

Job Title: HR Generalist

Location: Lekki Phase 1, Lagos, Employment Type: Full-time

Key Responsibilities

Recruitment and Onboarding:

  • Assist in sourcing and recruiting top talent for architectural, design, and administrative roles.
  • Coordinate onboarding processes to ensure new hires integrate smoothly into the company.

Employee Relations:

  • Serve as a point of contact for employee queries and concerns, fostering a positive work environment. Promote company culture and values through employee engagement initiatives.
  • Promote company culture and values through employee engagement initiatives.

HR Administration:

  • Maintain accurate and up-to-date employee records, ensuring compliance with legal and company standards.
  • Manage HR documentation, including contracts, letters, and performance reviews.

Payroll and Benefits Administration:

  • Support payroll processes by verifying attendance, leave records, and employee benefits.
  • Assist in resolving payroll-related queries in a timely manner.

Compliance:

  • Ensure compliance with Nigerian labor laws and company policies.
  • Assist in updating HR policies and employee handbooks as needed.

Training and Development:

  • Identify training needs and coordinate training programs for staff.
  • Monitor employee development and recommend growth opportunities.

Performance Management:

  • Support the implementation of performance evaluation systems.
  • Track employee progress and recommend actionable insights to managers.

Requirements

  • Education: Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Experience: 1-3years of experience in human resources, preferably within a architectural or constrction or design-focused organization.

Skills:

  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Proficiency in HR software and Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Excellent organizational and multitasking abilities.
  • Familiarity with Nigerian labor laws and HR best practices.

Additional Requirements:

  • Candidate must reside within Lagos Island or surrounding areas.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and adapt to changing priorities.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunity to grow within a reputable architecture and design company.
  • A collaborative and creative work environment.

How to Apply

Interested and qualified candidates should send their CV to: annconsult86@gmail.com using "Application for HR Generalist Role" as the subject of the email.
